Simple secret sharing (splitting) with pen & paper fallback recovery

 
This repository provides a simple t of n threshold secret sharing scheme for up to 9 participants. The secret can be revealed by a specified minimum number of participants (the threshold).

The secret owner (aka dealer) can split a secret into secret shares. The shares are create using the encryption technic Random Substitution Cipher. The shares are issued in recovery sets of shares. The individual shares do not reveal the secret. The owner of the secret then delivers the shares to the participants for safe storage.

The secret can later be recovered by combining a complete recovery set of shares. Combining shares can be done electronically or alternatively manually by using pen and paper.

For ease of use a web app has also been created. Once the web app is launched it can be saved locally and used offline. The offline usage capability can ensure that the app cannot transmit your secret elsewhere. When the app is permanently offline the need to trust that the web app will not steal secret data can be mitigated.

Create & distribute secret shares
Using the web app all the needed shares are easily created and distributed such that any threshold combination of participants can combine their shares from a common share set and recover the secret.

Therefore every participant will have one share in all the recovery share sets that the participant is included in.

Participants are identified by the upper case characters A-I. Recovery share sets are named using participant characters in alphabetical order. For example "BDE" is the recovery set including participant "B", "D" and "E". Individual shares consist of a list of as many numbers as there are characters in the secret.
(step is completed using the web app)

Deliver shares to participants
After share issuing and distribution the secret owner must deliver the shares securely to the participants. The shares can be delivered electronically or they can be printed and distributed physically.
(step is completed manually by the secret owner)

Recover secret
The secret can be recovered by combining all the shares in any given recovery share set.

When using the web app for recovery the shares must be typed or copy/pasted into the recovery form in the requested order. For recovery of the secret using pen and paper see explanation in the document "Secret Owner Information" that is issued together with the shares.
(step is completed using the app or manually by using pen and paper)
